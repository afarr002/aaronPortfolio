What's my dream job?

Wealthy philanthropist, helping to change the communities I grew up in and more just like them across the country.


What did I learn during quarentine?

I used the initial lock downs to learn about the hobbies I was interested in. I took classes on photography and photography editing. I learned about Investing and Day Trading, and I started businesses for disc dyeing and custom canvas prints. Most importantly, it's where I started my journey in full stack development that's led me here!


Why do I want to be a web developer?

Someone once told me that coding and web development is as fun and endless as you can be creative. It's a field where you have to continuously be learning and growing and developing new skills. I love the problems solving aspects of development, as well as the ability to build and create while collaborating with like minded people all over the world!
